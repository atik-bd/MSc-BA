# MSc-BA
This repository showcases a collection of academic projects, case studies, and practical assignments completed during my Master of Science in Business Analytics at Dublin Business School.
It highlights real-world applications of business analytics concepts, including data analysis, business intelligence, predictive modeling, sentiment analysis, and strategic decision-making frameworks.
Technologies and methodologies applied include Excel, Power BI, Python, SQL, and data visualization best practices.
This portfolio demonstrates my ability to transform data into actionable insights and deliver data-driven solutions to business challenges.
